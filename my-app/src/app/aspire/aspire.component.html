About Aspire/Inspire

The ASPIRE/INSPIRE program consists of a diverse group of students from Southern California high schools (ASPIRE) and California community colleges (INSPIRE). A collaboration between The Henry Samueli School of Engineering and the Donald Bren School of ICS, the program put student teams to work designing Smart Home projects compatible with the Internet of Things. From a Raspberry Pi Internet radio, to smart locks for the home, to a “magic mirror” that displays information on an overlay, to an intelligent refrigerator inventory system, the students set their sights high and completed prototypes of these ambitious projects in just two weeks.
